What is ITICS
Revenue-Driven Classification at Scale

Traditional systems assign one industry label per company. ITICS breaks each company into its actual operating segments with revenue percentages — a three-level taxonomy that evolves as the company does.

  • 🎯

    Revenue-Based Mapping

    Every segment gets a revenue share, classified as Pure Play (>50%), Quasi (20–50%), or Marginal (<20%).

  • 🔄

    Continuously Updated

    Updated with every annual and quarterly filing. New themes added annually as markets shift.

  • 🌍

    Global Coverage

    Developed and emerging markets worldwide, classified under one standardized framework.
